## OBJECTION HANDLERS (SHORT & PUNCHY)

### "I'm not sure I'm self-driven enough"
**Response:**
"Quick test: in the last 30 days, how many times did you train without someone 
telling you to? [listen] That's your answer. If you've done it, you have the 
discipline. My job is making sure you're doing the *right* things, not pushing 
you to show up."

### "This feels like it might be too hands-off for me"
**Response:**
"Fair. That's what the check-ins are for. Every week, I see what you did, 
how you felt, where you're progressing. I'm hands-off on timing but hands-on 
on programming. You'll get weekly feedback and adjustments. Not hands-off—
just asynchronous."

### "How fast do you respond?"
**Response:**
"Standard response is 24-48 hours on business days. Most questions get answered 
in your weekly check-in review anyway. If you need faster, there's a Priority 
Support add-on for $49/month—same-day weekday responses."

### "Isn't this basically a template program?"
**Response:**
"No—templates go to thousands of people. This is built for you. I look at 
your weekly data, your specific weak points, your equipment, your schedule. 
By week 4, this is completely personalized to you. That's why I adjust it 
every single week."

### "What if I don't see progress?"
**Response:**
"We have a 30-day guarantee. If you follow the plan, do the check-ins, and 
you're not seeing measurable progress (strength, reps, metrics, *something*), 
we'll either adjust the approach or part ways clean. I'm not here to take 
your money and ignore you."

### "It's expensive"
**Response:**
"For [Tier], you're paying [Price]/month. That's [Price/4]/week. If I was 
doing 1-on-1 coaching, this would cost you [3-4x more] and you'd be on my 
schedule. You're basically getting a coaching brain without the calendar 
friction. That's the deal."

### "Can I do monthly or do I need to prepay?"
**Response:**
"Month to month. But if you want to lock in 3 or 6 months, I offer a [10-20%] 
discount. Saves you money, helps me forecast. Either way works."

### "How do I know you're actually reviewing my check-ins?"
**Response:**
"You'll see it in my response. I reference specific workouts, specific metrics, 
specific things you mentioned. I'm not sending templated replies. That'll be 
obvious in the first week."
