# 14-Day Async Coaching Launch Plan

## Overview
Launch three async coaching tiers to existing network + new leads. Target: 5-10 paid signups in 14 days.

---

## Day 1-2: Setup & Messaging Prep

### Day 1: Internal Setup
- [ ] Create pricing page / landing page (or Stripe products)
- [ ] Set up Stripe checkouts for each tier
- [ ] Create intake form (Google Form or Typeform)
- [ ] Build email templates (welcome, check-in reminder, progress summary)
- [ ] Set calendar availability for consults (block 30 min slots)
- [ ] Document your SOP (check-in cadence, response times, guarantee)

### Day 2: Message Creation & List Building
- [ ] Create target list: past clients, warm leads, DM followers, email list
- [ ] Segment into tiers by training age/goals:
  - Tier 1: Complete beginners → Training Club pitch
  - Tier 2: Intermediate + self-driven → Independent Athlete Coaching
  - Tier 3: Advanced + high-touch needs → Independent Athlete Plus
- [ ] Write core messaging framework (keep under 3 versions, A/B later)
- [ ] Create 5 proof points / testimonials / before-afters to sprinkle throughout outreach

## Day 9-10: Phone/Consult Conversations

### Day 9: Schedule Consult Calls

**For people who replied positively:**
- Offer 2-3 time slot options
- Keep to 20-30 minute calls (not 1 hour)
- Use Calendly or similar for easy booking

**What to prep BEFORE the call:**
- Their application form (intake)
- Their training background
- 2-3 specific questions based on their goals

### Day 10: Run Consults (20-30 min each)

**Consult Agenda (always in this order):**

1. **Goals check (5 min)**
   - "Walk me through what you're trying to accomplish"
   - Listen for: clarity, timeline, specificity
   
2. **Training background (3 min)**
   - "How long have you been training?"
   - "What's worked or not worked for you?"
   
3. **Async fit assessment (5 min)**
   - "What appeals to you about async coaching?"
   - "What concerns do you have?"
   - **Red flags:** if they need daily hand-holding, suggest 1:1 coaching instead
   
4. **Tier recommendation (4 min)**
   - Based on answers above, recommend ONE tier (don't give all three)
   - Use this logic:
     - Beginner + budget → Training Club
     - Intermediate + self-driven → Independent Athlete (recommend this)
     - Advanced + wants nutrition/community → Independent Athlete Plus
   
5. **Close (3 min)**
   - "I think [Tier Name] is the best fit. Here's why..."
   - "First month is 30 days. If you're following the plan and doing check-ins, 
     you'll see progress. If it's not working, we'll adjust or part ways—no 
     contracts."
   - "You want to move forward?" (if yes → send link immediately)
   - "Any blockers or questions?" (address final objections)

## Day 11-12: Objection Handling + Close Scripts

### Common Objections & Responses

**"I'm not sure if I'm self-driven enough for async"**
- Response: "That's actually a great question. Let me ask: how many times in 
the last month did you hit the gym/train even when you didn't have a session 
scheduled? [Listen] That tells me you have the discipline. My job is to make 
sure you're doing the right things, not to motivate you to show up. The check-ins 
help with that."

**"How is this different from a template program?"**
- Response: "Good question. Template programs are generic—you get the same 
thing as 10,000 other people. This is personalized. I look at your weekly 
data, your specific gaps, and adjust your plan every week. By week 4, this 
becomes YOUR program, not a template."

**"What if I need to ask questions?"**
- Response: "You can message me anytime. Standard response is 24-48 hours on 
business days. If you need something faster, we can upgrade to Priority 
Support for $49/month. But honestly, most questions get answered in the weekly 
check-in."

**"It's kind of expensive"**
- Response: "For [Tier], you're getting a fully customized program + weekly 
adjustments + form feedback every month. That's $X/week (do math). If I was 
doing 1:1 coaching, this would be $600+/month for the same programming work. 
You're essentially getting the coaching brain without the calendar friction. 
That's the deal."

**"I've tried coaching before and it didn't work"**
- Response: "What didn't work about it? [listen] Okay. Here's how this is 
different: [specific difference based on their answer]. And look—we have a 
30-day guarantee. If you follow the program and do the check-ins and don't 
see progress, we'll figure out what's missing. I'm not going to just take 
your money and disappear."

**"Can I pay monthly or do I need to prepay?"**
- Response: "You pay monthly. But if you want to prepay 3 or 6 months, I 
offer [X% discount]. Saves you money and helps me plan."

## Contingencies & Adjustments

**If you get fewer than 3 signups by Day 10:**
- Increase DM volume to 20-25/day (Days 11-14)
- Add one more email to list (different angle: testimonial-driven)
- Consider slight price drop ($149 instead of $179) for first 5 clients

**If you get 8+ signups by Day 12:**
- Lock down pricing (no discounts moving forward)
- Close applications to "coming soon" list
- Use early clients as case studies for next launch

**If consult close rate is <40%:**
- Record a consult (with permission)
- Watch for friction in your script
- Adjust objection handling or tier recommendations
