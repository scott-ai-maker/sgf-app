# Async Coaching Pre-Launch Prep (3-6 Month Waiting Period)

Ready to launch the moment you get your NASM cert. Do these things NOW, activate the rest on Day 0.

---

## Phase 1: Content & Positioning (Do Now)

These require zero NASM cert and establish your authority before launch.

### Content to Create

- [ ] **5-10 posts about async coaching** (ideally published before launch, or queued)
  - "What async coaching looks like"
  - "How I program for independent athletes"
  - "5 mistakes people make training alone"
  - "Why you don't need weekly calls"
  - Save as drafts / schedule posts for launch week

- [ ] **Client intake form** (Google Form or Typeform)
  - Training age, goals, equipment, schedule
  - Injury history, sleep/stress/nutrition baseline
  - Save template, don't publish yet

- [ ] **Welcome email sequence** (3-5 emails)
  - Welcome + intake form
  - First training block introduction + video standards
  - Week 1 check-in reminder
  - Week 4 progress check
  - Save in Google Docs or email template folder

- [ ] **Check-in form template**
  - Session completion, performance notes, sleep, stress, metrics
  - Standardize this now (save a template)

- [ ] **Video library for form standards**
  - Record 5-10 example videos showing exercise standards
  - "How to submit form videos"
  - "What good form looks like on [exercise]"
  - Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Dropbox
  - These get sent to clients during onboarding

### Positioning & Messaging

- [ ] **Write your positioning statement** (2-3 sentences)
  - "I coach independent athletes who can execute their own training but need expert programming and accountability."
  - Use this in all marketing copy

- [ ] **Draft all copy** (landing page, DM scripts, emails)
  - Use templates from async-coaching-email-scripts.md (already created)
  - Customize for your voice
  - Store in Google Docs (not published yet)

- [ ] **Create one-liner for each tier** (memorize these)
  - Training Club: "Structured programs for self-driven athletes"
  - Independent Athlete: "Personalized coaching without the calls"
  - Independent Athlete Plus: "White-glove async coaching with nutrition"

### Build Your Audience (Low-Key)

- [ ] **Post consistently on socials** (1-3x/week)
  - Talk about training, coaching, fitness
  - Don't hard-sell yet, just build credibility
  - By launch, you'll have engaged followers to message

- [ ] **Email list growth**
  - Add a simple signup to your website / bio
  - Goal: 50-100 emails by launch
  - Use existing clients/followers

- [ ] **Document your coaching philosophy**
  - Write it down in a Google Doc
  - How do you approach program design?
  - How do you adjust based on client data?
  - When you get NASM, you'll lean on this + the cert for credibility

## Phase 2: Business Setup (Do Now)

### Financial & Legal

- [ ] **Stripe account** (set up now, configure later)
  - Don't create products yet, just have account ready
  - Link to bank

- [ ] **Business entity** (if not already done)
  - Sole proprietor, LLC, S-corp?
  - Talk to accountant; don't over-engineer this

- [ ] **Pricing locked in**
  - Training Club: $49-79/mo (decide on your specific price)
  - Independent Athlete: $179-249/mo
  - Independent Athlete Plus: $299-399/mo
  - Write it down; don't change it before launch

- [ ] **Think through your tax situation**
  - Self-employment tax, sales tax if applicable in your state
  - Get ahead of this with your accountant NOW while you have time

### Tools & Infrastructure

- [ ] **Email tool chosen** (ConvertKit, Flodesk, Mailchimp, etc.)
  - Set up templates folder
  - Create simple welcome automation (not published yet)

- [ ] **Client communication method decided**
  - Slack workspace, Discord, in-app messaging, email?
  - Set this up but don't populate yet

- [ ] **Calendar tool** (Calendly, Acuity, etc.)
  - Set up availability (block your actual availability)
  - Don't publish link yet

- [ ] **Check-in form tool** (Google Form, Typeform, Notion)
  - Create template now
  - Test it (go through it yourself)

- [ ] **File storage organized**
  - Google Drive folder structure for client plans, check-ins, etc.
  - Template plans ready
  - Video library organized

## Phase 3: Credibility & Proof (Do Now)

### Gather Social Proof Early

- [ ] **Reach out to past clients**
  - Ask for testimonials NOW (while you have relationship)
  - Store them in a doc
  - Use at launch ("I've coached X people through [goal]")

- [ ] **Document past wins**
  - Old client results, before-afters, testimonials
  - Create a simple "track record" doc
  - When you get NASM, you'll reference both cert + track record

- [ ] **Photo/video library**
  - Headshot for landing page
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - Training footage
  - Have these ready

- [ ] **NASM study plan**
  - Get your cert on a timeline (don't let it slip past 6 months)
  - Schedule exam date NOW
  - Treat it like a hard deadline

## Phase 4: Launch-Day Readiness (Do During Month Before)

### Landing Page / Sales Page

- [ ] **Domain + hosting** (if needed)
  - Buy domain NOW (99designs.com, Squarespace, etc.)
  - Don't build page yet, buy domain

- [ ] **Create sales page** (Month 5 or 6)
  - Copy from async-coaching-launch-plan.md
  - Customize with your story + NASM credential mention
  - Keep it simple: headline, why async, what you get, tier options, CTA

- [ ] **Stripe products created**
  - Three products: Training Club, Independent Athlete, Independent Athlete Plus
  - Create 1-month and 3-month/6-month options (annual = good retention)
  - Checkout link ready

### Readiness Checklist (Do in Month 5-6)

ONLY after you've passed your NASM exam:

- [ ] **Update all copy** to mention NASM certification
  - "NASM-Certified Personal Trainer"
  - Add to email signature, landing page, DM intro
  - Adjust positioning: "As an NASM-certified trainer with X years of experience..."

- [ ] **Publish landing page**
  - Sales page live on your domain
  - Links all working (Stripe, calendar, email signup)

- [ ] **Launch sequence ready**
  - Intake form live
  - Email welcome sequence built and queued
  - Check-in form template live
  - Video library uploaded and organized

- [ ] **Create first batch of 3 sample plans**
  - These are for consulting calls (show what clients get)
  - Beginner template
  - Intermediate template
  - Advanced template

- [ ] **Social media scheduled**
  - First week of launch posts scheduled
  - Announcement post ready
  - Email send list prepped (will send Day 1)

- [ ] **DM list prepared**
  - Exported contacts from Instagram/email
  - Organized by tier (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
  - DM scripts saved and personalized

- [ ] **Calendly live**
  - Available slots showing for consults
  - Auto-responder set: "Thanks for booking. This is a [20-min] consult about [tier]. Here's what to expect..."

- [ ] **Accountability partner assigned** (optional but helpful)
  - Someone to check in with on launch week
  - Track your daily DMs, reply rate, consults booked

## What NOT to Do (During Waiting Period)

- ❌ Don't launch before getting your NASM cert (you said 3-6 months, so wait)
- ❌ Don't create Stripe products yet (creates temptation to launch early)
- ❌ Don't reach out to leads yet (save them for launch day outreach)
- ❌ Don't hard-sell on socials (build audience, don't sell)
- ❌ Don't change your pricing strategy (lock it in now, don't second-guess)
- ❌ Don't wait for everything to be perfect (launching is better)
