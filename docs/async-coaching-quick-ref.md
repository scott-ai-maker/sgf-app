# Async Coaching — Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

## Pricing & Positioning (Copy This)

### Training Club
- **Price:** $49-79/month
- **Best For:** Budget-conscious, self-driven, beginners
- **Key Features:**
  - Customized plan
  - Progress tracking
  - Basic check-in support
- **Time Commitment:** ~45 min/month from coach

### Independent Athlete Coaching (FLAGSHIP)
- **Price:** $179-249/month ← **Recommend this one to most people**
- **Best For:** Intermediate+, self-driven, want accountability
- **Key Features:**
  - Personalized plan
  - Weekly check-ins + adjustments
  - Video form feedback
  - Messaging support (24-48h)
- **Time Commitment:** ~90 min/month from coach
- **Pitch:** "Same coaching brain, no calendar friction"

### Independent Athlete Plus
- **Price:** $299-399/month
- **Best For:** Advanced, wants nutrition + higher touch
- **Key Features:**
  - Everything in base, PLUS:
  - Nutrition targets + habit coaching
  - Faster messaging (same-day weekdays)
  - Biweekly plan deep dives
- **Time Commitment:** ~150 min/month from coach

### Add-Ons (High Margin)
- Technique Review Pack: $59-99 (5-10 videos)
- Nutrition Setup: $129-199 (one-time)
- Priority Support: $39-59/month
- 4-Week Focus Sprint: $149-249 (time-bound intensive)

## Tier Recommendation Logic (Use This in Consults)

### Ask These 3 Questions

1. **"How long have you been training?"**
   - <6 months → Training Club
   - 6-24 months → Independent Athlete
   - 2+ years → Independent Athlete Plus (or base if new to async)

2. **"Do you currently train without a coach?"**
   - No, never → Training Club (safer bet, lower price)
   - Yes, sometimes → Independent Athlete (you have discipline)
   - Yes, consistently → Independent Athlete Plus (high confidence)

3. **"What's your biggest pain point right now?"**
   - "I don't know what to do" → Training Club
   - "I know what to do but need adjustments/feedback" → Independent Athlete
   - "I want everything: plan, nutrition, accountability, faster support" → Plus

**Then recommend just ONE tier** (not all three).

## Effective Hourly Rate (Check Your Pricing)

**Formula:** (Monthly Price - Costs) / Monthly Time in Hours = Effective Rate

**Examples:**

| Tier | Price | Time/mo | Costs/mo | Effective Rate |
|------|-------|---------|----------|----------------|
| Training Club | $65 | 0.75h | $18 | **$62.67/hr** |
| Independent Athlete | $215 | 1.5h | $18 | **$131.33/hr** |
| Plus | $350 | 2.5h | $18 | **$132.80/hr** |

**Target:** $120-150/hour minimum (otherwise reconsider pricing).

## Red Flags in a Consult (May Not Be Right Fit)

- ❌ "I need you to motivate me to go to the gym"
  - Response: Recommend 1:1 coaching instead
- ❌ "I need daily check-ins"
  - Response: Upgrade to Plus or suggest 1:1
- ❌ "I'm not sure I can stick to this without someone watching me"
  - Response: "Async isn't for everyone—let's talk about 1:1 options"
- ❌ "Can we do Zoom calls every week?"
  - Response: "That's my 1:1 coaching product, let me tell you about that instead"

**If 3+ red flags:** politely decline and keep them for future 1:1 waitlist.

---

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't lower prices mid-launch (you'll regret signaling weakness)
- ❌ Don't over-customize onboarding (keep it repeatable)
- ❌ Don't respond to every message immediately (sets bad precedent)
- ❌ Don't promise things outside your scope ("I can get you abs in 8 weeks")
- ❌ Don't take more than 8-10 clients in first cohort (you don't have time)

---

## What TO Do Instead

- ✅ Stick to pricing for first 14 days (offers credibility)
- ✅ Same onboarding process for everyone (predictability)
- ✅ Automated check-in reminders (consistency)
- ✅ Under-promise, over-deliver (build raving fans)
- ✅ Qualify hard in consults (only take good fits)
